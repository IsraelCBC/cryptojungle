# CryptoJungle — Claude Context

## Brand Identity
**CryptoJungle** is Israel's #1 crypto media and education platform, operating since 2017.

## Tone & Voice
- Authoritative but accessible
- Written primarily for a **Hebrew-speaking crypto audience**
- Conversational enough for newcomers, credible enough for professionals
- Never preachy or overly technical without context
- Do **not** mix with BDCC's formal/academic tone or any other brand voice

## Key Channels
| Channel | Details |
|---|---|
| Website | cryptojungle.co.il |
| YouTube | Main video content — news, analysis, interviews |
| Podcast | Regular audio episodes |
| Telegram | 75,000-member community |
| Conferences | Annual flagship events in Israel |

## Content Types
- Breaking crypto news and analysis
- Educational guides (beginner → advanced)
- Opinion pieces and market commentary
- Conference coverage and recaps
- Social media posts (Telegram, Twitter/X, Instagram)

## Recurring Tasks in This Repo
- **Content repurposing** — transform long-form content into social clips, threads, posts
- **Weekly analytics reports** — traffic, YouTube, community growth metrics
- **Community updates** — Telegram announcements and pinned messages
- **Content research** — trend monitoring, competitor tracking, topic ideation
